From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a list of countries by economic freedom that shows the countries and regions from two reports on economic freedom.

  • The Economic Freedom of the World Index is a report published by the Fraser Institute in conjunction with the Economic Freedom Network, a Canadian group of independent research and educational institutes in 90 nations and territories worldwide.[2]
  • The Index of Economic Freedom is an annual report published by The Heritage Foundation and The Wall Street Journal in the United States. Countries and regions are assessed as free, mostly free, moderately free, mostly unfree, or repressed.[3]
